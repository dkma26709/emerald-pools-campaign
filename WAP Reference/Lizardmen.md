## Faction Rules

**Cold-Blooded** — roll 3D6 for Leadership tests, discard the highest.

**Predatory Fighter** — on a natural 6 To Hit in CC, generate an extra Attack (roll To Hit/Wound normally). Bonus attacks don't generate further attacks. Unit with PF must take Ld test to restrain pursuit (modified by combat result margin).

**Blood Frenzy** — after inflicting one or more unsaved Wounds, the model/unit gains Frenzy until end of next turn.

**Jungle Poisons** — Poisoned Attacks on all missile attacks from javelins and shortbows.

**Chameleon** — shooting attacks against the unit suffer -1 To Hit.

**War Gauntlets** — two hand weapons with Impact Hits (1) and Magical Attacks.
