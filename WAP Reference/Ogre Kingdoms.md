## Faction Rules

**Ogre Charge** — On-foot models with this rule gain Impact Hits (1) on successful charge. Units with ranks add Rank Bonus to Impact Hits Strength. Count as one extra rank for Steadfast on charge turn.

**Beneath Contempt** — Gnoblars have Expendable. Other Gnoblars also treat Gnoblars as Expendable.

**Bicker** — Units with US 5+ not in combat/fleeing roll D6 each turn; on 1, cannot move or shoot.
