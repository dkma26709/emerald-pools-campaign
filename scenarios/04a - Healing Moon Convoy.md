# Scenario 4a — Healing Moon Convoy

*~October 2025 — simultaneous with Gnawtooth Depths*

---

## Armies

**Lizardmen** (Daniel, campaign) vs **Bretonnia** (stand-in)

## Objective

Rescue Null'Zotl from the Bretonnian convoy. Bonus: slay Pierre de Carrou.

## What Happened

Skinks, Razordons, and Salamanders surrounded the convoy. Bretonnian movement mistakes left the wagon exposed — destroyed easily, freeing Null'Zotl. Null'Zotl was charged by Pierre de Carrou's unit — won the combat and slew de Carrou (bonus objective achieved). Skrr'ipt and Saurus were charged by Bretonnian cavalry and took heavy losses. Ripperdactyls helped turn that fight. Skrr'ipt was wounded: rolled Robbed — lost a newly acquired arcane item.

## Result

**Lizardmen win.**

## Consequences

- **Null'Zotl rescued. Bond restored.**
- **Null'Zotl:** Gains Extremely Predatory Fighter.
- **Skrr'ipt:** Wounded — lost an arcane item (Robbed).
