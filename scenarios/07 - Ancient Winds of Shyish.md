# Scenario 7 — Ancient Winds of Shyish

*~October 2025*

---

## Armies

**Lizardmen** (Daniel, campaign) vs **Orcs & Goblins** (Jesper, campaign)

## Objective

Protect the underworld entrance. The pillars must remain active. Bonus: no Greenskins within 3" of the entrance at game end.

## What Happened

Disastrous start — Razordon and Salamander units lost Turn 1. The Orcs deactivated all but one pillar. Orc cavalry failed a charge against decoy Skinks, left exposed to Null'Zotl who charged and routed them, then pursued into the Goblin wizard unit — both fled off table. Skrr'ipt and Skinks reactivated pillars. Temple Guard beat the Black Orcs but were then slaughtered by the Troll hero, who nearly reached the underworld entrance — stopped only by his own Stupidity. Kroq'Debug and Saurus fought off the Orcs, killed the Orc general in a challenge (no wounds taken), then destroyed a catapult.

## Result

**Lizardmen win.** Bonus objective achieved — no Greenskins within 3" of the entrance.

## Consequences

- **Lizardmen:** Underworld entrance sealed. +1 loot die modifier. Skaven threat from below contained.
- **Kroq'Debug:** Undefeated in challenge again. Zero wounds taken across the entire campaign.
- **The underworld:** Sealed — for now. Whatever lurks below remains contained.
