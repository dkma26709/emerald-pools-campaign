# Scenario 5 — Priest in Pieces

*~October 2025*

---

## Armies

**Tomb Kings** (Hjalte, campaign) vs **Orcs & Goblins** (stand-in)

## Objective

The Liche Priest (slain in Scenario 1) was scattered across the battlefield in six pieces. Recover them all to restore the priest at full power.

## What Happened

The Tomb Kings recovered all 6 pieces.

## Result

**Tomb Kings win — all 6 pieces recovered.**

## Consequences

- **Tomb Kings:** Liche Priest fully reassembled and improved. Per scenario rules: 6 pieces = priest returns with a free roll on the experience table.
