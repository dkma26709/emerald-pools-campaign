# Scenario 3 — Broken Beach Landing

*~September 2025*

---

## Armies

**Bretonnia** (Anders, campaign) vs **Orcs & Goblins** (Jesper, campaign)

## What Happened

Bretonnian archers were ineffective early — couldn't wound the Orc Troll hero even with flaming arrows. A single cavalry unit turned the entire battle once it arrived, winning a combat it should have lost and routing the Orc army.

## Result

**Bretonnia wins.**

## Consequences

- **Bretonnia:** Beachhead secured.
- **Bretonnian general:** Wounded — gained Stupidity. (This is the general who becomes Gaston le Furieux.)
- **Orc Troll hero:** Levelled up — gained a special rule allowing extra movement when failing Stupidity tests.
