# Scenario 4b — Gnawtooth Depths Demolition

*~October 2025 — simultaneous with Healing Moon Convoy*

---

## Armies

**Lizardmen** (Daniel, campaign) vs **Skaven** (stand-in)

## Objective

Seal the Skaven tunnels. Kroq'Debug in solo command (Skrr'ipt and Null'Zotl at the Convoy).

## What Happened

The Lizardmen achieved all objectives on Turn 1 — rules were adjusted mid-game to increase difficulty. Skaven shooting missed entirely, then the army was enveloped by Skinks, Raptadons, Terradons, Ripperdactyls, and Chameleons. Skaven characters were shot down. Stormvermin engaged by Saurus + Kroq'Debug and pummelled despite numerical advantage. Total rout even with amended rules.

## Result

**Lizardmen win — tabled Turn 3.**

## Consequences

- **Lizardmen:** Tunnels sealed. Skaven threat neutralised. Banner of Sotek claimed (bonus objective).
- **Kroq'Debug:** Continued his undefeated record. No wounds taken.
