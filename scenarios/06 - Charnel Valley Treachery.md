# Scenario 6 — Charnel Valley Treachery

*~October 2025*

---

## Armies

**Tomb Kings** (Hjalte, campaign) vs **Bretonnia** (stand-in) + **Dwarves** (stand-in) + **High Elves** (stand-in)

Four-way Battle Royale. Tomb Kings allied with Dwarves; Bretonnia allied with High Elves.

## Objective

The Tomb Kings needed to break through the Charnel Valley. Bonus: control the shrine.

## What Happened

The Dwarves barely moved, got stalled. Bretonnian Pegasus Knights took out the Dwarven cannon. Bretonnians held off Tomb Kings chariots and skeleton warriors. High Elven Swordmasters and War Lion Chariot annihilated skeleton archers and reached the catapult. The Tomb Scorpion nearly stole the High Elf fort but was contested just in time. Every army held their starting fort — nobody broke through.

## Result

**Stalemate — Tomb Kings fail.**

## Consequences

- **Tomb Kings:** +0 Trail. Charnel Valley not secured. Bonus objective (shrine control) not achieved.
- **Narrative:** The valley is blocked. The Tomb Kings must find another route — leading to the Portal of Twisted Paths.
