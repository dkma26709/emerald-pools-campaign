# Scenario 2 — Healing Moon Monastery

*~August 2025*

---

## Armies

**Lizardmen** (Daniel, campaign) vs **Bretonnia** (stand-in)

## What Happened

The Lizardmen attempted to secure more golden tablets. Kroq'Debug ground through the monks but was too slow. Null'Zotl's Kroxigor unit lost to spearmen and fled off table. Null'Zotl was captured by the Bretonnians. Skrr'ipt escaped alone. The Bond was separated.

## Result

**Bretonnia wins.**

## Consequences

- **Lizardmen:** No Trail Progress. No tablets secured.
- **Null'Zotl:** Captured. The Bond is broken — Skrr'ipt is alone.
- **Narrative:** This sets up the Healing Moon Convoy rescue mission.
