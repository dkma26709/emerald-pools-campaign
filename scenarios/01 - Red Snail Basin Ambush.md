# Scenario 1 — Red Snail Basin Ambush

*~June 2025*

---

## Armies

**Tomb Kings** (Hjalte, campaign) vs **Lizardmen** (Daniel, campaign)

## Objective

The Lizardmen held golden tablets — ancient records of the Old Ones. The Tomb Kings sought to claim them.

## What Happened

The Tomb Kings' archers devastated the Skink screen early. Raptadons carrying a tablet were caught by a Tomb Scorpion. Null'Zotl and the Saurus Warriors fought through skeletons, chariots, and archers, but ran out of time to escape with the tablets. Null'Zotl killed all but the Tomb Prince in close combat. The Liche Priest was slain — but may rise again.

## Result

**Tomb Kings win.** The golden tablets were claimed.

## Consequences

- **Tomb Kings:** Tablets secured. The Tomb Prince can now read them — they point west of the World's Edge Mountains.
- **Lizardmen:** No Trail Progress. The tablets are lost.
- **The Liche Priest:** Slain, but this is not necessarily permanent for the Tomb Kings.
