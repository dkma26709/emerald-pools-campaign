# Scenario 8 — Desert Fever

*~October 2025*

---

## Armies

**Bretonnia** (Anders, campaign) vs **Dwarves** (stand-in, replacing Dogs of War)

## Objective

Claim the Treasury building and the Spear of Antarhak within.

## What Happened

Dwarves subbed in for an absent player. The Bretonnians fought hard but couldn't dislodge the Dwarves from the Treasury building. No Spear of Antarhak was claimed.

## Result

**Bretonnia loses.**

## Consequences

- **Bretonnia:** +0 Trail. Treasure remains unclaimed.
- **Narrative:** The Bretonnian expedition ends on a low note. Anders transitioning to Empire.
