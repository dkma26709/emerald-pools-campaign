- Carries Skrr'ipt on his shoulder (deploy together)

---

## Deadlock Club (5 Tiers) — *the weight of inevitability*

A colossal stone club whose blows carry the stillness of inevitability. For a breath before impact, prey hangs transfixed — and when prey tries to run, the stillness holds it a moment longer. The club's identity is singular: **nothing escapes.**

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Stillness Begins** | Causes Fear. Enemies breaking suffer -2" flee distance (min 2"). | Kills first enemy model with the club in a victory | |
| 2 | **Deadlocked** | Enemies in base contact suffer -1 Attack (min 1). | Breaks an enemy unit in CC | |
| 3 | **Heavy Step** | Unit gains +2" pursuit and overrun distance. | Unit runs down a fleeing enemy | |
| 4 | **Crushing Stillness** | Enemies breaking roll 3D6 discard two highest for flee (replaces T1). | Singlehandedly breaks a Monstrous Infantry+ unit | |
| 5 | **Weight of Inevitability** | Fleeing enemies auto-caught on pursuit. Once per battle, all attacks auto-hit. | Campaign climax / Pool restoration | |

## Bond-Stone (3 Tiers) — *the pendant that refuses to break*

A small jade pendant Skrr'ipt strung onto Null'Zotl's harness with his own claws. It pulses when the priest is in danger. The Bond-Stone is entirely about *taking hits for someone else*.

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Silent Sentinel** | 4+ intercept attacks on Skrr'ipt within 3". Resolved vs Null'Zotl's T and saves. | Ends a battle within 3" of Skrr'ipt, both alive | |
| 2 | **Quiet Vow** | Range extends to 6". Once per battle, force-intercept on failed roll. | Intercepts an attack that would have killed Skrr'ipt | |
| 3 | **Unbroken Bond** | When Skrr'ipt would die: D6, 3+ survives at 1W, Null'Zotl takes D3 (no saves). | Absorbs a blow that would have killed Skrr'ipt | |

| Level | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
