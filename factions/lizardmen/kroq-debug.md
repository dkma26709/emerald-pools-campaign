## Starting Rules

- Blessed Spawning of Huanchi
- **Blessed Mark of the Old Ones** — 3 re-rolls per battle (To Hit, To Wound, armour save, or Ld test)
- **Silent Authority** — Saurus within 6" gain Immunity (Psychology) in the first round of combat
- **Immune to Psychology.** May never voluntarily Flee as a charge reaction.
- Runtime Executioner and Runtime Aegis both **dormant**

---

## Runtime Executioner (5 Tiers) — *the predator's blade*

A macuahuitl of black stone edged with glyphs that shimmer and change. Each blow falls with terrible certainty, as if correcting faults invisible to others. The weapon cares about one thing: the head of the snake.

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **First Compilation** | +1 Attack. Re-roll failed To Hit rolls against enemy characters. | Kills his first enemy character in CC | |
| 2 | **Runtime Active** | +1 Initiative. AP(1) vs enemy characters. | Defeats a named character in a challenge | |
| 3 | **Extended Runtime** | +1 Weapon Skill. Re-roll failed To Wound vs enemy characters. | Kills a character with higher WS | |
| 4 | **Full Compile** | AP(2) vs characters (replaces T2). Enemy characters -1 To Hit vs Kroq'Debug in challenges. | Completes a multi-scenario objective | |
| 5 | **Edge of Realities** | Ignore Armour vs characters in challenges. Once per battle, HKB in a challenge. Slay causes Panic at -1 Ld. | Campaign climax / Pool restoration | |

## Runtime Aegis (3 Tiers) — *the ancient scales*

A small obsidian-and-jade shield, paired with the Runtime Executioner as the second half of a matched set from the First Days. The Aegis does not stop blows — it disperses them across surfaces older than the jungle itself.

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |

| 1 | **Sentinel Initialised** | Re-roll failed armour saves. | Endures a battle where he took wounds in CC and was still standing | |

| 3 | **Zero-Exception Build** | When reduced to 0 Wounds, stay at 1 (once per battle). Regeneration (5+). | Oldblood ascension or campaign climax | |

## The Duel (3 Levels) — *the predator's reputation*

Not what the weapon does — what *he* does. Kroq'Debug does not duel for honour. He targets the head of the snake because that is efficient. The prey's death ends the fight; the rank and file are irrelevant.

| Level | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Implacable Silence** | Enemies in base contact suffer -1 Leadership. | Wins a challenge without taking a wound | |
| 2 | **Apex Predator** | Causes Fear against enemy characters. | Defeats a second named character across the campaign | |
| 3 | **Marked Prey** | Slain faction characters suffer Terror instead. CC attacks may target characters in base contact outside challenges. | Character-kills decisive across multiple scenarios | |

## The Directive (3 Levels) — *the warleader's command*

Kroq'Debug inspires by example, not by words. The army watches what the Compiler does and adjusts. Saurus instinct sharpens around him; the cold-blooded discipline of the spawning pools runs deeper where he stands.

| Level | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Privileged Command** | Friendly Saurus within 6" gain +1 Leadership. | Unit anchors a battle line against multiple charges | |
| 2 | **Optimised Predator** | Predatory Fighter triggers on 5+ (instead of 6). Bonus attacks can't trigger further PF. | Slays 3+ models in a single CC phase | |
| 3 | **Hard-Coded** | Once per battle, all friendly Lizardmen within 12" are ITP and re-roll failed Ld tests until end of turn. | The army executes a coordinated action that wins a scenario | |
