# Skrr'ipt, Chanter of Silent Glyphs

**Skink Priest** — Wizard Level 1, Lore of Shadow

| Profile | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
|---------|---|----|----|---|---|---|---|---|-----|
| Skrr'ipt | 6 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 2 | 4 | 1 | 5 |

---

## Starting Rules

- Blessed Spawning of Huanchi
- **Lore of Shadow** — not normally available to Skink Priests; Huanchi's nature channelled as magic. This is his differentiator.
- Rides on Null'Zotl's shoulder (deploy together)
- Async Rod and Eye of Hidden Steps both **dormant**

---

## Async Rod (5 Tiers) — *the unstable chant*

A staff of pale jade whose glyphs writhe like shadows at dusk. Skinks call it "the unstable chant" — but when it speaks, it speaks with Huanchi's silence. As the rod awakens, it channels not just the Lore of Shadow but Huanchi's own magic — unique spells no other priest knows.

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **First Shadow** | D6 each phase: 4+ = +1 to cast. Must re-roll natural 6s while active. | Casts a spell contributing to a scenario victory | |
| 2 | **Jaguar's Veil** | Huanchi spell: *Augment 18" CV7+. Shooting vs target suffers -1 To Hit.* | Magic protects an allied unit from shooting | |
| 3 | **Shadow Glyphs Align** | Threshold improves to 3+. Re-roll-6s penalty removed. | Survives a Miscast, or casting decisive across multiple phases | |
| 4 | **Hunter's Mark** | Huanchi spell: *Hex 24" CV7+. Friendly units +1 To Hit vs target in CC.* | Magic enables a charge or combat that breaks the enemy | |
| 5 | **Veil of the Jaguar-God** | Auto +1 to cast. Bearer and unit gain Chameleon (-1 To Hit shooting). | Channels the Huanchi-Key at a pool, or secures a milestone | |

## Eye of Hidden Steps (3 Tiers) — *the obsidian disc*

A small obsidian disc carved with a jaguar's pupil, hung on a leather thong. It shows Skrr'ipt the next step — sometimes a full heartbeat before it happens.

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **First Glimpse** | Once per battle, force one enemy charge to roll 3D6 discard highest. | Foresight was narratively decisive | |
| 2 | **Phantom Step** | Once per battle, one friendly LM Infantry/Skirmisher unit makes free M-value move. | Foresight saved or repositioned an allied unit | |
| 3 | **Huanchi's Patience** | Huanchi spell: *Augment 12" CV8+. Unit that didn't move gains +1 TH and +1 TW in CC.* | Holding position becomes as dangerous as charging | |

---

## The Chatter (3 Levels) — *the noise that hides the truth*

The constant chattering is not nervous energy — it is a weapon. The noise hides Skrr'ipt's real intent, disorients enemies trying to counter his magic, and amplifies when the guardian is near. The bond with Null'Zotl is woven through this track.

| Level | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Constant Chatter** | Auto-pass Panic within 3" of Null'Zotl. | Both survive a battle with the bond under pressure | |
| 2 | **Feint and Strike** | Once per phase, after enemy dispels: immediately cast a different spell using same dice. | Magic phase involved misdirection | |
| 3 | **Amplified Noise** | Feint and Strike usable twice within 6" of Null'Zotl. Enemy Wizards within 18" suffer -1 to dispel. | Pair's presence disrupts an enemy magic phase | |

---

## The Shadow (3 Levels) — *the jaguar's stealth*

Huanchi's gift to Skrr'ipt: the ability to be somewhere else. Hidden deployment, mid-battle repositioning, and finally the ability to bring his entire unit into the shadow with him.

| Level | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Jaguar's Prowl** | Scouts when joined to a Skink Skirmisher unit. | Deployment was the decisive factor | |
| 2 | **Shadow-Stalk** | Once per battle, reposition within 12", behind terrain, join new unit. | Reposition turned the battle | |
| 3 | **Shadow Walk** | Skrr'ipt and his unit deploy as Scouts (any unit, not just Skirmishers). | Deploys a non-skirmisher unit in an unexpected position | |
