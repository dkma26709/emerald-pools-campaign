# The Rosbach Expedition — Army Status

The expeditionary force from **Ofenstadt**, an iron-mining city built into the scarred slopes of Kessler's Ridge — south of the Empire proper, hard against the Tilean border. Furnaces burn day and night; soot blackens the rooftops; the people are hard, blunt, and practical. Ofenstadt iron is not the finest, but it is cheap, plentiful, and reliable.

The Counsel of Ofenstadt has charged **Friedrich Rostbach** with leading an expedition south into the Land of the Dead to find **Aldric Brunnholt**, a prominent member of the city's merchant guild who vanished along with his caravan beyond the borders of civilised lands.
