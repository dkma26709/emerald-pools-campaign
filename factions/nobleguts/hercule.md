## Foebreaker (5 Tiers) — *the knight's glaive*

A brutal glaive of jagged iron, far too heavy for a human to lift. Hercule treats it with the reverence a Bretonnian lord reserves for his ancestral blade. The weapon is not elegant. But it is carried with such purpose that it has started to cut as though it were.

| 3 | **Virtue's Reach** | Fear in challenges (Terror if already). Re-roll To Wound in challenges. | Wins vs equal or higher WS | |
| 4 | **Virtue's Edge** | +2I (replaces T2). AP(2) vs chars (replaces T2). Enemy -1 TH in challenges. | Completes a quest | |

## Errant's Surcoat (3 Tiers) — *the oath made cloth*

A tabard of blue and white, stitched by Hercule's own hands after the beastman battle. Each oath fulfilled adds a thread. The surcoat rewards the knight who faces his foe openly.

| Tier | Name | Effect | Earned when... | Status |
