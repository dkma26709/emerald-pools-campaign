# The Emerald Pools

*Somewhere in the deepest jungles of Lustria, where the canopy swallows the sun and the rivers run green with ancient magic, there lies a site of immense power — the Emerald Pools. Sacred spawning grounds of the Old Ones, lost for millennia beneath root and ruin.*

*Four armies have come searching. Each has their own reasons. None intend to share.*

---

## The Factions

### The Blessed of Huanchi — Lizardmen
*The cold-blooded servants of the Old Ones march with singular purpose. Led by the Scar-Veteran Kroq'Debug and guided by the prophetic bond between the Skink Priest Skrr'ipt and the savage Kroxigor Null'Zotl, the Lizardmen seek to restore what was lost before the younger races defile it. The Pools are theirs by right of creation. They will not ask twice.*

### The Legions of the Barren King — Tomb Kings
*From the deserts of Nehekhara, the Tomb Kings have sent an expedition westward. Ancient texts speak of the Pools' power to restore what death has claimed. The golden tablets — stolen, fought over, and recovered — point the way. The dead do not tire, and the Barren King's patience is eternal.*

### The Nobleguts — Ogre Kingdoms
*Where there is power, there is something worth eating. The Nobleguts are a mercenary tribe drawn by the Great Maw's hunger and rumours of ancient magic. Led by the Bruiser Hercule, they follow the trail left by the Greenskins before them — who lacked the stomach to finish the journey.*

### The Imperial Expedition — Empire
*The Empire's interest is newer but no less determined. Following reports from Bretonnian scouts who fought and bled in these jungles, an Imperial force prepares to march. What the knights began with lance and prayer, the Empire will finish with steel, gunpowder, and discipline.*

---

## The Campaign

This is a **Warhammer Fantasy Battles** narrative campaign using the Warhammer Armies Project rules. Four players. Vibes-based storytelling — scenarios and consequences are decided collaboratively between sessions based on what happened on the table.

Each faction tracks **Trail Progress** toward the Emerald Pools. Victories, bonus objectives, and narrative events push armies closer to the final confrontation.

**Campaign Status:** 11 battles fought. 4 scenarios planned. The Pools remain undiscovered.

---

## For the Players

This repository is the living record of the campaign. Faction rosters, character sheets, scenario write-ups, and the campaign reference all live here.

**To contribute:** You have collaborator access. Edit files on GitHub directly, or clone the repo and work locally. All changes to `main` go through a pull request — make your edits on a branch, open a PR, and it will be reviewed and merged.

If you are unsure how, ask Daniel. A companion app is in the works.
